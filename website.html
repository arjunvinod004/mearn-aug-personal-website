    NEWS LEO ON TARGET AS PSG ROUT LILLE

    The Parisians chalked up another win to bring their tally from the opening three Ligue 1 games to a perfect nine. On this occasion, Leo and his PSG teammates brought the curtain down on the Matchday 3 action with a 7-1 away triumph over Lille. Leo completed the full 90 minutes and fired home a right-footed finish to claim his third goal of the season, while he provided an assist for the team’s opening goal.


    LEO HITS BRACE IN CURTAIN-RAISER

    PSG opened their Ligue 1 campaign with a 5-0 away triumph over Clermont that saw Leo bag a brace, the second of which was an overhead kick. This double means that Leo has netted three times in his first two competitive outings of the campaign.


    PSG CLAIM ANOTHER LIGUE 1 WIN

    Leo completed the full 90 minutes as PSG ran out 5-2 winners over Montpellier at the Parc des Princes in the sides’ Matchday 2 meeting. This win means that the Parisians have now collected six points from their opening two games.

    ABOUT
