Leo got one of the goals in Paris Saint-Germain's 4-5 victory in the friendly against Riyadh Season from Saudi Arabia. The Argentine opened the scoring for the Parisian team three minutes into the match.


LEO NAMED IN PSG SQUAD AFTER ARGENTINA WORLD CUP TRIUMPH

Leo has been named in the Paris Saint-Germain squad after having lifted the World Cup in Qatar with Argentina. The Parisians face Angers on Wednesday in the sides' Ligue 1 Matchday 18 encounter. PSG lie top of the league standings on 44 points and hosts Les Scoïstes currently sit bottom on eight points.



TRIBUTE TO LEO ON HIS RETURN TO PSG

Leo returned to training with Paris Saint-Germain this Wednesday after proclaiming himself world champion with Argentina in the 2022 World Cup in Qatar. On his return to Paris, he received a tribute from all his teammates and French club workers. “Thank you very much to my teammates and to all the people at the club for the welcome they gave me. We are back and looking forward to continuing to meet the objectives of this season, now with PSG", Leo said after receiving an award and walking the "champion's corridor" carried out by his teammates at the Camps de Camps training ground. LEO, NAMED BEST PLAYER IN THE WORLD CUP FOR QATAR

Leo was named the best player in the World Cup in Qatar after winning the trophy after the victory against France. The Argentine, who was named the best player in the final, was also awarded this award in five of Argentina's seven matches. Leo, who finished the World Cup in Qatar with seven goals and three assists, also ended up being World Cup MVP in Brazil in 2014.
